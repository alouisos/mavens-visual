Our Mission

Work with researchers to conduct the highest quality clinical research, addressing important questions relating to human health and disease.

Cambridge Clinical Trials Unit has been awarded full registration by the UKCRC CTU network. We collaborate with Trials Teams to conduct 37 active Trials/Studies, which includes 26 CTIMPs, 10 Non CTIMPs as well as supporting 1 device trial. Learn More...

Services

We deliver world class clinical trials of all phases in all aspects: from grant proposals, study design, developing protocols, obtaining necessary approvals, quality control, data management, trial conduct and management, final analysis, to reporting of outcomes and publications.
